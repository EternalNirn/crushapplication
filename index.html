FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How long does review take?

Anywhere from 3 days to the heat death of the universe. We recommend not checking your phone every 5 minutes. (You will check your phone every 5 minutes.)

Can I re-apply after rejection?

Yes. The standard re-application cooldown is "a respectable amount of time." Exact duration is undefined but you'll know.

Do I need prior experience?

No. However, knowing how to share a dessert, laugh at yourself, and send a timely meme will significantly strengthen your application.

What does "approved" actually mean?

We're still figuring that out. Legally: nothing. Emotionally: a lot. We'll keep you posted.

Is my personal information safe?

Your data is stored securely in a private notes app and may be replayed to the applicant's best friend multiple times.

What if I don't know my star sign?

You may select "None (I'm normal)." This will be treated as a point in your favour by exactly half of our review panel.
